Welcome To WE Club

Who WE Are

With the motto “From Me To We”, WE club recognizes and utilizes the benefits of working collaboratively. As such, the club divides its time between fostering a positive community amongst the students at BCIS as well as raising funds for education in rural China. In the spirit of ‘together we will be able to achieve more’, the club will often partner and support other student-led service initiatives that are happening throughout the year.
